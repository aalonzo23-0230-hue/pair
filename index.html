About Us

Who we are?

Rural Bank of Paete Inc. is a community-based bank dedicated to delivering reliable, affordable, and accessible financial services to individuals, families, farmers, and small businesses.

Our Story

Founded in 1966, our bank has been a steady partner in financial growth, helping generations of clients manage their savings, invest for the future, and sustain local enterprises.

Our Mission and Vision Statement

Our mission and vision guide our purpose and direction as we provide banking services with integrity, staying true to our values and long-term goals for the community.

Mission

To promote financial inclusion by providing safe, accessible, and sustainable banking solutions to our community.

Vision

To be the most trusted and responsive rural bank in Laguna and neighboring provinces.
