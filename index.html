About Us

Who we are?

Rural Bank of Paete Inc. is a proud community-based bank committed to delivering reliable, affordable, and inclusive financial services to individuals, farmers: fisherfolk, and micro-entrepreneurs in Laguna and neighboring areas..

Our Story

Founded in 1966 our bank has been a pillar of strength for the local economy We are passionate about empowering our clients, whether it's helping you start your savings journey or funding your farm or small business.

Our Mission and Vision Statement

Our mission and vision guide our purpose and direction as we provide banking services with integrity, staying true to our values and long-term goals for the community.

Mission

To promote financial inclusion and growth in rural communities by providing safe, simple, and sustainable banking solutions.

Vision

To be the most trusted and accessible rural bank in Laguna and Southern Luzon, driven by integrity, innovation, and community care.
